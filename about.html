About Us

Our Story

Welcome to A.L.P.S – Aquinas Learning Portal System! We are the students of Aranzan, Section 9-E, St. Thomas Aquinas. The idea of this website was born from a challenging school project where we aimed to create a dynamic, interactive, and informative platform for our school community.

Making this website has been a rollercoaster: sleepless nights, endless debugging, and yes, some serious stress—but also creativity, learning, and fun. ALPS is not just a website; it’s our journey, our teamwork, and a showcase of what can happen when dedication meets technology.

Our Purpose

The goal of A.L.P.S is to provide an informative and interactive experience for students, teachers, and visitors. Here you can explore:

  • Subjects
  • Facilities
  • The history

Our vision is to make learning accessible, engaging, and fun—showing the spirit of St. Thomas Aquinas through modern technology.
