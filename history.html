Our History

The Diocesan Shrine and Parish of Nuestra Señora de Aranzazu in San Mateo, Rizal, established in 1596 by Augustinians and later managed by Jesuits, is the only parish dedicated to this Basque Virgin Mary title in the Philippines.



Founded in 1705, the church houses a miraculous image recognized as a protector against calamities. In 2025, it was elevated to a National Shrine, marking its enduring spiritual and historical significance.
